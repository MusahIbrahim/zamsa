Welcome to Zamsa Academy

Zamsa offers a world class learning environment for early childhood and basic education in Accra, Ghana.

It is the vision of Zamsa to nurture children towards success and good leadership. Offering a world class learning environment for early childhood and basic education for children from all walks of life at affordable fees has been our mission. We strive to inculcate the values of academic excellence, quality service, discipline, friendship and tolerance in our students
